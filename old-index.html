1969 Dodge D100

The day I inherited this truck, I drove it 500 miles from California to Utah. That was a brilliant move that seized the engine in the middle of the desert. I spent the rest of my checking account having it towed home, and then I began saving and rebuilding the truck to be my daily driver one day. After 10 long months of waiting, my new engine arrived, so I installed it, broke it in, and then after 30 minutes of driving, it seized. I was out of funds at that point, so I sold the rig, gave my wife back her money, and decided to try again when I had full-time employment.

Toy Story Spanish Curiosity

I was 16 years old when Toy Story 3 came out in 2010. I loved it, especially when Buzz Lightyear went into Spanish mode. A couple years later I learned Spanish as a missionary for my church, and one day I remembered the Spanish Buzz scenes. I rewatched the movie and enjoyed the scenes anew, but then I got curious about what Spanish Buzz spoke in the Spanish translations of the film, so I looked into it and made a little video on the topic. Turns out he just speaks Spanish. My little YouTube video exploded in views once Toy Story 4 was announced for some reason. I haven't had a video go viral since.
